Blue Chicken

Finished blue chicken breast recipe

This recipe is the fastest and most effective way to cook BLUE CHICKEN!!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil, crumbled
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano, crumbled
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme, crumbled
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Wash chicken well and dry.
  3. Combine herbs, salt and lots of freshly-crushed black pepper.
  4. Sprinkle chicken pieces with seasonings.
  5. In non-stick skillet, melt butter and brown chicken on both sides.
